TeamNest was built with Next.js, React and TypeScript on the frontend, FastAPI and PostgreSQL on the backend, FastAPI WebSockets for real-time channels, DMs and notifications, and a LlamaIndex + Pinecone + Groq RAG pipeline for the AI assistant, packaged with Docker and deployed to Render and Vercel. The product was delivered across five sprints: authentication and profiles, real-time messaging and notifications, organizations with social and task features, paid plans with global search and the audit log, and the RAG AI assistant with final UX polish. Infrastructure built early — WebSockets, Cloudinary uploads, the role/permission model and the Pinecone index — compounded across the later sprints and carried the product to feature-complete.
