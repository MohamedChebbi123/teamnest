This chapter walks through how TeamNest was built. It opens with the development environment and the full technology stack — frontend, backend, database, real-time layer, AI stack and DevOps tooling — then unfolds the five sprints that delivered the product, each with its own backlog, C4 component diagram, class diagram, key sequence diagrams, UI screens and a closing review and retrospective. Together the sprints carry the project from authentication and real-time messaging through social and task features, paid plans and search, and finally to the RAG-powered AI assistant and launch readiness.
